HCHS Fine Arts



Band is a great way for student for student to learn a new skill. To join band you must have experience with reading sheet music, know how to play an instrument, and following directions. Also to join band you will need lots of free time for performances and after school practice, and such during concert season you sit and play music to prepare for concerts and festival. You also have a conductor to watch and pay attention to so he can tell you how high or low to play,and when to play. Band is also a academic class which is for a grade and has a finale. Band is also an after school activity some days depending on your section on when you have sectionals.
