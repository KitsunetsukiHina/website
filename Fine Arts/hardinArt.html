HCHS Fine Arts



Mrs. Hager Droke is in charge of the National Art Honor Society. Art class is full of opportunities to improve, as well as discover, your skills in drawing, painting, sculpting, and much more! The National Art Honor Society also helps you improve your skills. NAHS sometimes does face painting at the football games and at the tailgate party's. The only requirements to join the club are you must have taken Art 1 and made a good grade in that class. The class can be both easy and fun if you pay attention to Mrs. Droke's instructions.
