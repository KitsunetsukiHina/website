"Hardin county band is a fun activity for high schoolers of all ages it encourages physical activity outside or inside. There are multiple sections in marching band these sections are divided into woodwinds,brass,and percussion. Each instrument is very important to the band they play an important roll to the song. The drum majors conduct the band during marching season. marching season ends after football season. Marching and has a ranking system p1,p2,and p3 p1 means your in the show p2 means you shadow someone p3 means you need practice. During marching season we have after school practice on Tuesday and Thursday.Marching band also has two other directors Mr white,and Mr Tyson,Mrs Tyson directs the color guard. Marching season last from August to late October early November.
